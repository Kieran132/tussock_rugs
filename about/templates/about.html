TUSSOCK:

An area of grass, thicker than that around it; a grassy knoll; Mother Earths carpet

Tussock was born in 2022 when creator Jasmine Price could not find the right rug for her flat. Instead of buying a factory-made copy, she embarked upon the process of making a unique topographical piece inspired by her home town in Shropshire.

A year later, and one rug has become a house full of yarn, and a business dedicated to recreating the beauty of the UK countryside in tufted form. Everything Tussock creates is inspired by the natural world and our rugs, tapestries and artworks add a soft touch to any interior.
